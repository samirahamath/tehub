Chapter 01

An engineering
team that treats
code as craft.

TEHUB was founded in 2018 by three software engineers and a product designer who had spent years freelancing across New York, Berlin, and London, and who were tired of how often a great software idea was reduced to bloated, off-the-shelf templates. Our agency is small on purpose — a core group of seven developers, one DevOps architect, two product managers, and a network of trusted security and design consultants.

We design for simplicity. We build for scale. — Mira Halden, Founder & Chief Architect

What that means in practice: we take on fewer parallel projects per quarter, we keep the same technical lead on an account from the first kickoff call to the final deployment, and we deliver clean, tested, self-documenting code. Clients have described our approach as "expensive to hire, but cheap to scale." That is, on reflection, exactly how we want to be described.
